ABOUT


Ahmed Alnajjar


Professor Ahmed Abdulazziz Alnajjar is an academician, lecturer, trainer, researcher, consultant, and TV producer/host.
He is the first from the Arab world to ever join the Social Psychology Organization and in recognition of his scholarly achievements and academic leadership he was honored as one of the “Who’s Who in the World” (1999).


He earned his Ph.D. in Psychology from the University of Durham, UK, and currently chairs the Psychology Department of the United Arab Emirates University. As a multi-awarded speaker and resource person in a host of national, regional, and international forums, he was bestowed upon the prestigious titles, Ambassador of Knowledge in 2015 awarded by HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid and Ambassador of Tolerance and Peace awarded in Cairo in 2016. His dedication to lifelong learning and continuing education have largely broadened the latitude of his professional expertise which cut across the diverse and multidisciplinary fields of Social Psychology, Leadership, Criminal Psychology, Industrial-Organizational Psychology, Family and Adolescence, Decision-making and Problem Solving, and Positive Psychology. He is a member of several prestigious international associations such as the American Psychological Association, American Counseling Association, Afro-Asian Psychological Association, International Leadership Association, American Holistic Health Association, as well as national organizations such as the Committee of Gifted People in the UAE and the Emirates Psychological Association (EPA).
